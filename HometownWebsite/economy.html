Doing business and investing in Cebu

Proudly stands as one of the Philippines’ economic powerhouses, Cebu’s strong economy fosters a nurturing environment for families especially in Cebu City. Cebu City is prominently fueled by its rapidly expanding Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) and Information Technology (IT) sectors, which have not only attracted local talents but have also become a magnet for professionals from around the country and the world.

Beyond BPO and IT, Cebu’s economy thrives on a wide range of industries, including manufacturing, shipping, and tourism, contributing to a well-rounded economic ecosystem. The province’s economic dynamism elevates the overall vitality and plays a pivotal role in providing a wide array of job opportunities, making it a desirable destination for both job seekers and entrepreneurs. Cebu offers not only career prospects but also a wealth of experiences, from cultural and recreational activities to educational opportunities, making it an attractive place for individuals and families to explore, work, and reside.

Cebu was a natural meeting point for merchants from various parts of the globe in search of markets and supplies.
